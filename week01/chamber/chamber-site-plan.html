Site Name

Lagos Chamber of Commerce - This name represents a business community in Lagos, Nigeria, which supports local enterprises.

Site Purpose

The Lagos Chamber of Commerce site aims to:

  • Promote business networking and collaboration.
  • Provide information about economic growth and opportunities.

Scenarios

Visitors might ask:

  • What business events are happening this month?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's leadership?
  • What are the economic statistics for Lagos?

Color Schema

The website will use the following colors:

  • Dark Blue (#003366) - For headers and navigation.
  • Light Gray (#f5f5f5) - For background.
  • Gold (#ffcc00) - For highlights and buttons.

Typography

Font choices:

  • Montserrat - For headings.
  • Open Sans - For body text.
